About

I am an eternal learner. Being a highly motivated and sociable team player, I reckon that it is vital to have a big picture in mind and only then look at the details. This mindset helps me to come up with the most rational and effective solution to almost any problem that comes up. I am also passionate to learn new things and gain experience in fields other than my major.

Even though I started learning to code recently, I am dedicated to improve day by day to finally reach my goal of becoming a software developer. Since the middle school I had an interest in IT sphere: I truly enjoyed IT lessons, passionately did my homework on algorithms basics and wrote simple programs in Visual Basic.

Later, I switched school and, unfortunately, I was discouraged to keep learning programming by my new IT teacher. He was convinced that IT is not a suitable subject for a girl to take seriously. Another reason I did not pursue my passion was an internal fear that I am not that smart and not good at math to become a programmer. Now, decades later, I finally got rid of those irrational fears and am ready to chase my childhood dream of becoming a software developer.
